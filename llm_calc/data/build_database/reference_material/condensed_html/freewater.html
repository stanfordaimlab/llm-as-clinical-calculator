FORMULA

Free water deficit, L = (% total body water, fraction)*(Weight, kg)*([Current Na/Ideal Na] – 1)

where % total body water (TBW) is:

  • Adult male: 60% (i.e., use 0.6 in the equation)
  • Adult female: 50% (0.5)
  • Elderly male: 50% (0.5)
  • Elderly female: 45% (0.45)
  • Child: 60% (0.6)
